ABOUT

A developer who treats the small details
as the actual product.

I'm a front-end developer and student at Usmanu Danfodiyo University Sokoto, where I lead the technical side of my faculty's student-union web presence. What started as a simple informational site became a hands-on education in service workers, manifest files, and the dozens of small decisions that separate a webpage from an app.

I care about the parts most visitors never consciously notice: the timing of a hover state, the weight of a shadow, whether a page still works on a slow connection. My process is deliberate and a little obsessive — I'd rather rebuild a component three times than ship something that merely works.
